About Me

I am a research scholar at Dept of Computer science & engineering, IIT Madras under Dr. C. Chandra Sekhar. I am associated with the Speech and Vision Lab. My research interests include Machine Learning, Kernel Methods for Pattern Analysis and Game Theory . My current research focus is on Multi-view learing. Prior to my MS, I have done B.Tech in CSE from Meerut Institute of Engg. & Technology, Meerut.
