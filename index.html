About Me

Aditya has been in domain of data science and artificial inteligence for more than 11 years. He started his journey in data science in 2012 when he began MS in Machine learning specialisation at IIT Madras, Chennai. Thereafter He has worked in Fintech industry for around 8 years in companies like Citibank and Oneassist. As a Chief Data Scientist at leading NBFC, Aditya is responsible for overseeing Machine Learning models and data products to ensure a data driven culture.
